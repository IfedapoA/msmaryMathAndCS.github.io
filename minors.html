Currently Offered Minors

Computer Science

"The Mount’s computer science minor will provide you with coding and technical skills that will give you a leg up when applying for jobs and is a great complement to many majors. Pair the minor with a biology major and pursue careers in the fields of computational biology and bioinformatics."

Source: MSMU Computer Science Major/Minor

Minor Requirements: 18 Credits

Core Requirements: 12 credits
CMSCI 120 - Introduction to Computer Science I, Credits: (3)
CMSCI 125 - Introduction to Computer Science II, Credits: (3)
CMSCI 254 - Data Structures and Algorithms, Credits: (3)
CMSCI 256 - Principles of Software Development, Credits: (3)

Elective Requirements: 6 credits
CMSCI XXX Computer Science Elective Credits (3)
CMSCI XXX Computer Science Elective Credits (3)

Source: Mount St. Mary's Course Catalog

Cybersecurity

"The Mount’s cybersecurity minor offers a knowledge base in the design, operations and security of computer networks. Pair the minor with a criminal justice major and become a fraud analyst for law enforcement, an economics degree to pursue a career protecting financial data, or a political science major and work with federal agencies to keep our nation safe."

Source: MSMU Cybersecurity Major/Minor

Minor Requirements: 18 Credits

Core Requirements: 9 credits
CMSCI 120 - Introduction to Computer Science I, Credits: (3)
CYBER 260 - Networking Administration, Credits: (3)
CYBER 261 - UNIX and Windows Operating Systems, Credits: (3)

One additional CMSCI/ CYBER course: 3 credits
Choose one additional course from the list in the Course Catalog.

Two additional electives: 6 credits
Choose two elective courses from the list in the Course Catalog.

Source: Mount St. Mary's Course Catalog

Data Science

"The Mount’s data science minor can be a great complement to many majors. Pair the minor with a computer science degree and become a database administrator for a technology company, an accounting major and reconstruct financial records for banking institutions, or a mathematics degree and become an information scientist for a government or research organization."

Source: MSMU Data Science Major/Minor

Minor Requirements: 18 Credits

Core Requirements: 9 credits
DATA 200 Introduction to Data Science, Credits: (3)
DATA 210 Exploratory Data Analysis, Credits: (3)
DATA 220 Machine Learning Models, Credits: (3)

Elective Requirements: 9 credits
Choose three electives from the list in the Course Catalog.

Source: Mount St. Mary's Course Catalog

Mathematics

"The Mount’s mathematics minor can be a great complement to many majors. Pair the minor with a cybersecurity major and protect financial institutions from cybercrime, a health science degree to pursue a career as a biomedical researcher, or a business major to become an operations research analyst."

Source: MSMU Mathematics Major/Minor

Minor Requirements: 20 Credits

Core Requirements: 11 credits
MATH 211 - Mathematical Thinking, Credits: (3)
MATH 247 - Calculus I, Credits: (4)
MATH 248 - Calculus II, Credits: (4)

Elective Requirements: 9 credits
MATH XXX - MATH Elective 200-Level or higher, Credits: (3)
MATH XXX - MATH Elective 200-Level or higher, Credits: (3)
MATH XXX - MATH Elective 200-Level or higher, Credits: (3) or DATA 200 Introduction to Data Science, Credits: (3)

Source: Mount St. Mary's Course Catalog
